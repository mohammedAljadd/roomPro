About RoomPro

RoomPro is a modern, intuitive room booking and management system designed to streamline scheduling for meeting rooms and maintenance in office environments.

Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for users to:

  • Easily view available rooms
  • Book meeting spaces quickly
  • Manage recurring cleanings and maintenance schedules
  • Track personal reservations and bookings
  • Administer rooms and equipment efficiently

Key Features

  • Booking calendar with conflict detection
  • Recurring events support (cleanings, maintenance) with holiday exclusions
  • Role-based access: Admin panel for room and equipment management
  • User-friendly Angular frontend paired with a robust Spring Boot backend

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Spring Boot (REST APIs, security, data management)
  • Frontend: Angular (SPA with dynamic calendar views)
  • Database: Relational database (PostgreSQL)
  • Scheduling: RRule plugin for recurring events and exclusions
